Taniti Island is proud to offer a wide range of amenities to ensure your stay is comfortable, convenient, and memorable. From leisure and adventure to accessibility and spirituality, we've got you covered.

General Visitor Amenities

  • Island-wide free Wi-Fi zones
  • Public restrooms and beach showers
  • Tourist information centers
  • ATMs and currency exchange booths
  • Phone charging stations

Recreational Amenities

  • Snorkeling and scuba gear rentals
  • Boat and kayak rentals
  • Beach volleyball courts
  • Hiking and nature trails with guides
  • Family picnic and BBQ areas

Dining & Shopping

  • Oceanfront restaurants and cafes
  • Local markets with island-grown produce
  • Vegan and gluten-free dining options
  • Souvenir and artisan gift shops

Accessibility Features

  • Wheelchair-accessible beaches and pathways
  • Accessible restrooms and seating areas
  • Signage in multiple languages
  • On-request visitor assistance services

Spiritual & Religious Centers

  • Taniti Island Mosque – Daily prayers and Friday Jummah services
  • Taniti Synagogue – Shabbat services and holiday observances
  • Taniti Christian Church – Non-denominational services on Sundays and holidays

All worship centers are open to visitors and are within a short distance of Taniti City. Respectful attire and behavior are appreciated.
