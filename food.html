Whether you're craving fresh seafood, classic American comfort food, or flavorful pan-Asian dishes, Taniti offers a rich variety of dining options. Here are 10 of our top spots:

Ocean Flavor

Known for its fresh mahi-mahi and jasmine rice bowls, Ocean Flavor is a beachfront favorite with sunset views.

Address: 12 Shoreline Blvd, Taniti City

Phone: (800) 555-5555

Island Catch

Catch of the day specials with wild rice, coconut sauces, and ocean-to-table freshness.

Address: 88 Coral Lane, Taniti Harbor

Phone: (800) 555-0000

Reefside Grill

This breezy dockside eatery serves grilled tuna steaks, sticky rice, and tropical fruit glazes. A local favorite.

Address: 7 Dockside Drive, Taniti Port

Phone: (800) 555-1111

Tide & Table

Elegant island dining with sesame-crusted marlin, bamboo rice, and ocean views from every seat.

Address: 45 Marina Walk, South Taniti

Phone: (800) 555-2222

Blue Lagoon Dining

Outdoor seating over crystal water, grilled snapper with island rice, and cold coconut drinks.

Address: 98 Lagoon Way, Taniti East Shore

Phone: (800) 555-3333

Burger Haven

Grilled burgers, crispy fries, and milkshakes just like home — with a tropical twist.

Address: 33 Sunset Strip, Taniti City

Phone: (800) 555-4444

Sunset Diner

Retro vibes, bottomless coffee, and all-day breakfast with island-fresh eggs and pancakes.

Address: 210 Palm Avenue, Taniti City

Phone: (800) 555-6666

Taniti Grillhouse

Classic steaks, BBQ ribs, and loaded baked potatoes — perfect after a day in the sun.

Address: 55 Flame Rd, Taniti Central

Phone: (800) 555-7777

Noodle Grove

A fusion of Thai, Vietnamese, and Indonesian flavors in a cozy garden setting.

Address: 5 Lotus Street, North Taniti

Phone: (800) 555-8888

Eastern Breeze

Enjoy sushi rolls, spicy Korean BBQ bowls, and Thai green curry with beachside seating under lantern-lit palms.

Address: 18 Bamboo Bay Rd, East Taniti

Phone: (800) 555-9999

Prefer to Cook Yourself?

If you'd rather prepare your own meals, Taniti has several convenient 24/7 food outlets:

  • SunMart Supermarket – Central Taniti City
  • TropicGro Supermarket – Near the airport
  • Green Basket Market – Small neighborhood grocery
  • Island Roots Grocery – Organic and local products
  • QuickStop Convenience Store – Open 24/7 near the bus terminal
