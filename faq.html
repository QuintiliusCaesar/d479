What type of power outlets are used on Taniti?

Taniti uses 120-volt outlets, the same as in the United States. Bring an adapter if your plugs or voltage differ.

Can I drink alcohol on the island?

Yes. Alcohol is available, but it cannot be sold or served between midnight and 9:00 a.m.

What is the legal drinking age?

The legal drinking age is 18, though the law is not strictly enforced.

Do people on Taniti speak English?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, especially in tourist areas. English is less common in rural areas among older residents.

What kind of medical facilities are available?

There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Is Taniti safe for tourists?

Violent crime is rare. However, pickpocketing and petty theft have increased slightly with tourism. Be mindful of your belongings.

Are there any national holidays to be aware of?

Yes. Taniti has many national holidays. Tourist attractions and restaurants may close, so plan ahead accordingly.

What currency is used on Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar. Many businesses also accept euros and yen. Currency exchange is available at banks, and major credit cards are widely accepted.
